About me

This page represents my professional growth from paper-roots to front-end development.

I started my journey as a print graphic designer (CS6) and moved on to digital design and e-commerce in 2016.
After creating web-sized pictures, my path took me to CMS-based webpages. Currently I manage a Magento e-commerce store with 50k products.

In December 2020 I decided to dive further into coding and found SheCodes, a company that teaches front-end development to women.
